Eswatini

Eswatini (formerly Swaziland) is a small, landlocked kingdom in Southern Africa, known for its rich traditions, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality. The country has a strong monarchy and vibrant cultural festivals like Umhlanga (Reed Dance).
